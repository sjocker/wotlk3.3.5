About Addons DPS list

Death Knight -> Blood -> mdps

Difficulty: Medium

Intro:

Blood DPS focuses on strong single-target damage using Heart Strike and scales extremely well with Armor Penetration in late game (ICC gear). It also provides strong self-healing and survivability compared to other DPS specs.

Single Target Priority

1. Keep diseases up (Icy Touch + Plague Strike)

2. Heart Strike

3. Death Strike

4. Death Coil (Runic Power dump)

5. Dancing Rune Weapon on cooldown

- Use Death Coil to avoid Runic Power capping

______________________________

AoE

1. Death and Decay

2. Pestilence

3. Blood Boil

4. Death Coil

Priority of stats

1. Armor Penetration (hard cap = 1400)

2. Strength

3. Hit = 8% (263 rating)

4. Expertise = 26 (214 rating)

5. Critical Strike

6. Haste

______________________________

Notes

- Cap Hit and Expertise first

- Armor Penetration is best in late game

- Strength scales all abilities

- Do not overcap Hit or Expertise
