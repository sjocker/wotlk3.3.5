Difficulty: Medium

Intro:

Survival Hunters are a damage-dealing specialization for the Hunter class in World of Warcraft. In patch 3.3.5, known as Wrath of the Lich King, Survival Hunters are known for their ability to deal high burst damage from range. They have a variety of abilities that allow them to adapt to various situations, including the ability to lay traps or to temporarily increase their attack speed. In both PvE and PvP environments, Survival Hunters excel as long-range damage dealers, able to take down their enemies from a safe distance. They are also able to provide helpful utility to their allies through the use of their traps and other abilities.
