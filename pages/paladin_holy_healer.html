Difficulty: Medium

Intro:

Holy paladins are powerful healers in Wrath of the Lich King patch 3.3.5, able to restore health to themselves and their allies with a variety of spells. They are also able to buff their allies with various auras, increasing their survivability on the battlefield. In addition to their healing abilities, holy paladins have access to a number of utility spells, such as crowd control and defensive abilities, which make them a valuable asset in any group. One of their key abilities is Holy Shock, which allows them to deal damage or heal their allies, depending on the situation. Holy paladins are also able to protect their allies with their Divine Shield ability, making them a formidable force in any situation.
