Intro:

Enhancement shamans are a powerful damage-dealing class in World of Warcraft, specifically during the Wrath of the Lich King expansion in patch 3.3.5. They excel at dealing damage to a single target and have a variety of abilities that allow them to deal damage over time. Enhancement shamans are known for their ability to quickly and efficiently dispatch their enemies, making them valuable assets in both PvE and PvP content. They also have access to several utility abilities, such as crowd control and party buffing, that make them valuable members of any group or raid. In addition, enhancement shamans have access to a variety of spells and abilities that enhance their physical attacks, allowing them to deal high amounts of melee damage.
