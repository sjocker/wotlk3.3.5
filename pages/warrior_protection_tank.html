Intro:

Protection warriors in World of Warcraft's Wrath of the Lich King expansion (Patch 3.3.5) are a formidable tanking class, capable of withstanding heavy damage and controlling the battlefield with their strong defensive abilities. They have a range of tools at their disposal to mitigate incoming damage, including shields, shields, and powerful shouts that can reduce damage taken by nearby allies. In addition to their defensive capabilities, protection warriors also have a number of offensive abilities, including a variety of strikes and charges that can be used to control the flow of battle. Whether standing at the front line, taking the brunt of the enemy's attack, or leading the charge into combat, protection warriors are an essential component of any successful raid or dungeon group.
